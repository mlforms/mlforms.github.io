Paracentesis kit
skin marker
gown
bouffant
sterile glovesx2
drape/sterile towels
chlorhexidine
6cc syringe for lido
20/50cc syringe for paracentesis
25" needle for lido
4x4s
urinal to waste extra fluid
urine cup for sterile specimen

Steps
use US to mark abd site w marker
sterilize area
open kit and drop everything onto it while chlorhex is drying
put urinal & specimen cup within reach
wear goggles/bouffant
open gloves
doff gown & gloves
poke with needle
remove needle and set aside
use syringe to drain from safety centesis needle
