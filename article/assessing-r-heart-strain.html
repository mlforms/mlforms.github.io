Assessing R Heart Strain

Created At：2022-09-29 14:02 Updated At：2024-01-05 21:15


A lateral RV wall measurement of < 0.5 cm suggests acute pulmonary hypertension (PH), > 0.5 cm suggests chronic PH. This measurement should be made from the subcostal view at end diastole.

RV dilation is nonspecific, Cor Pulmonale and PE will essentially look the same.
