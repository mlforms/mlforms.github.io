Arterial Line supplies

Created At：2022-12-31 12:31 Updated At：2024-04-08 15:21

A line
Suture
Scalpel/scissors
Sterile gloves x2
4x4
Tegaderm
Chlorhexidine
Wrist support
Lac kit or kelly clamps
Towel/Chuck for blood
Sterile US gel
US probe cover
Eyewear
PPE gown, booties
Silk tape to hold arm
Mayo stand
+/- Primed A-line tubing

SETUP
Pt arm positioned/taped
Chuck under arm
Sterilize site
Open lac kit sterilely
Dump suture onto field
Dump A-line onto field
Dump probe cover onto the field
Dump 4x4 onto field
Put US gel on probe
Put sterile gel on pt's arm
Have the US depth and M-line set before getting sterile
Don gloves, grab US probe, grab A-line

  • Asking for help holding the arm or silk tape
  • Have somewhere to toss the a-line sharp once inserted
  • Have suture ready at bedside (needle driver & curved suture is easiest)

TROUBLESHOOTING

  • If line is coming out of arm, consider detaching the tubing
