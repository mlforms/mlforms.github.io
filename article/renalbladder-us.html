Hydronephrosis



Chronic hydronephrosis will lead to thinning of the parenchyma.

Hallmark of severe nephrosis is the connection of the major with the minor calyxes and pelvis.
Renal fluid should be anechoic. If there are echos in the fluid, assume it's blood/pus.

It's easy to miss mild hydronephrosis.
Don't set the gain too high or it could bleed over and obscure the hydro. Use color dopper to see if fluid is blood vessels or urine.

If there is extra-renal pelvis dilation, that's okay as long as intrarenal pelvis is not dilated.
