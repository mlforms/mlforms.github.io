If you see A-lines and lung sliding, this signifies an absence of interstitial syndrome, alverolar consolidation, and pleural effusion at that specific area.

  • True B lines go all the way to the bottom of the screen.
  • B lines can be indicative of PNA, pulmonary contusion, pulm fibrosis, pulm infection
  • B lines on both sides indicates diffuse process like pulmonary edema
