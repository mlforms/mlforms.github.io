Pitfalls

  • Not asking for help holding the arm for jerky patients.
  • Pt with CP was very jerky and I lost the IV. Probably should've secured the IV with tegaderm before trying to draw blood.
  • Not putting down a towel/chuck for blood drops.
  • Underestimating how shallow 1-2mm really is and overshooting by several mm. For the really shallow ones my angle should be like 10 degrees not 30 degrees.
